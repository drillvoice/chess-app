Privacy Policy

Effective: 2025-08-16

Chess Logger (“the App”) records your chess practice and imports game data from Lichess when you connect a Lichess account. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

Data we collect

  • Account data: Email address if you sign in (Firebase Authentication).
  • App data you add: Practice logs, notes, goals, timestamps.
  • Lichess data you request: Username and game metadata (e.g., time control, color, result, timestamps). We do not collect your Lichess password.
  • Technical data: Basic diagnostics and aggregate performance logs from our hosting platform (e.g., request timestamps, IP at request time) used for reliability and abuse prevention.

How we use data

  • Provide core features (saving logs, showing stats, importing Lichess games).
  • Prevent abuse and ensure reliability.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We use service providers to run the App (e.g., Google Firebase for auth, database, and hosting). Providers process data on our behalf under their terms.

Data retention

Your practice logs and linked Lichess data are kept until you delete them or your account. Backups may persist for a limited period.

Your choices

  • You can add, edit, or delete your logs in the App.
  • You can disconnect Lichess at any time.
  • You can request account deletion by contacting us (see below). Deletion removes your account and associated data from our production systems after processing.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. Do not use the App if you are under the minimum age in your country without parental consent.

Security

We use industry-standard measures appropriate to our size and the nature of the data. No method is 100% secure.

International transfers

Data may be processed in countries where our providers operate (including outside your country).

Changes
